myCart Dallas: Giving Back

This Thursday, myCART Dallas employees picked up their trash bags and got to work! Every Thursday, myCart Dallas employees take the afternoon to head out and give back--often by volunteering at local homeless shelters, soup kitchens, as highway trash pickers, or as mentors for low-income schools in the Dallas area. At myCart Dallas, we know that we're all lucky every day to have what we have, and we try to take every opportunity to give back. We know our employees work hard and have busy schedules that make it even more difficult for them to take time out of their day to help others, and so we've continued to try to find ways to open up these opportunities for anyone who is interested. If you'd like to join myCart Dallas' Giving Back Thursdays, feel free to reach out--whether you're an employee or just looking for a way to take part, we'd be more than happy to have you!.
